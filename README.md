This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer financial behavior, asset strength, and risk exposure using a structured banking dataset.
The dashboard is designed to deliver executive-level insights into customer segmentation, deposit-loan balance, credit usage patterns, and overall financial stability.#

Key Objectives of the Dashboard
1. Analyze customer demographic and occupational distribution
2. Evaluate financial strength through income and deposit trends
3. Assess loan exposure and credit card usage
4. Identify high-risk customer segments using Risk Weighting
5. Compare loyalty segments based on financial contribution

Dashboard Overview
1️⃣ Customer Segmentation Analysis
Customers are segmented by:
1. Loyalty Classification
2. Occupation
3. Nationality
4. Age Group

2️⃣ Financial Strength Analysis
The dashboard evaluates:

1. Average Estimated Income
2. Total Bank Deposits
3. Total Bank Loans
4. Loan-to-Deposit Ratio
5. Properties Owned

3️⃣ Credit Behaviour Insights
The dashboard examines:
1. Credit Card Balance trends
2. Number of Credit Cards held
3. Relationship between credit usage and income
4. Loan exposure across loyalty segments#

4️⃣ Risk & Exposure Assessment
1. Using the Risk Weighting column, the dashboard:
2. Identifies high-risk age segments
3. Compares loan exposure across customer tiers
4. Highlights segments where loan-to-deposit ratio is elevated
5. Detects potential financial instability patterns

📈 Key Insights Derived
Premium loyalty segments contribute the highest deposit volume.
Higher income customers tend to maintain stronger deposit balances.
Certain age groups demonstrate higher average risk weighting.

Customers holding multiple credit cards show increased credit expoure.

Loan-to-deposit ratio provides a quick stability indicator of the portfolio.
